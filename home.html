A little history

Lagos, is the largest city in Nigeria and the second most populous city in Africa with a population of 15.4 million as of 2015. The city has been described as the cultural, financial and entertainment of Africa, and is a significant influence on commerce, entertainment, education, technology, tourism, art and fashion. Lagos was formed in 1967 and is commonly described as the Centre of Excellence. Click here to learn more.
